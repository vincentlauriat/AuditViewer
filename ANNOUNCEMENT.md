## Hacker News (Show HN)

**Title:** Show HN: AuditViewer – AI that turns any company into a sourced research dossier

**Body:**
AuditViewer turns a single command — `/audit-report Tesla` — into a complete, sourced research dossier: history, market sizing, technology, pricing, competition, financials, and outlook.

It's several parts sharing one versioned "machine contract": an AI audit skill (the engine), a web viewer, and native apps for macOS, iPhone/iPad, and Apple TV (the last two are read-only readers). The skill runs inside Claude Code or Gemini.

The design goal was trustworthiness over fluency. Every quantitative claim carries a source link and a publication date; sources are tagged Official/Analyst/Press; data older than a year is flagged; and the 5–10 most important figures are cross-checked against at least two independent sources. It's instructed never to fabricate a number.

The Mac app is signed, notarized, and auto-updates via Sparkle. Everything is MIT-licensed.

Repo: https://github.com/vincentlauriat/AuditViewer

Happy to answer questions about the machine contract, the multi-agent vs. solo execution modes, or how it stays consistent across Claude and Gemini.
